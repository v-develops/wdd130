ASTRONOMIC EVENT CALENDAR

December

December 12th (New Moon)
8:32 pm will be the best time to observe the stars, since there will be no moonlight.
December 14th (Meteor shower)
Under ideal conditions, it will be possible to see up to 150 meteors per hour!
December 22th (Solstice)
This will be the shortest day in the North Hemisphere, but the longest in the South Hemisphere.
December 27th (Full Moon)
Our natural satellite will be beautifully located in the Auriga constellation!
